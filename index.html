About Me

After 12 years of dedicated service, I concluded my military career with an honorable discharge. In the Army, I specialized in artillery, a role markedly different from my current pursuit in the tech industry. My interest in technology has deep roots, sparked in childhood by gaming and early experiments with Visual Basic. Although becoming a professional developer once seemed beyond my reach, leaving the Army marked the turning point to follow my passion for technology.

While I am naturally reserved in my personal life, I thrive on collaboration in professional settings, finding great satisfaction in achieving milestones as a team.

I try to learn new things everyday, and have a legimate desire to program cool things. I tend to enjoying solving problems, and find great satisfaction in watching a project evolve over time. As I become more experienced as a professional developer, I hope to find ways to leverage Machine Learning as well.
