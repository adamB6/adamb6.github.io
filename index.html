About Me

After 12 years of dedicated service, I concluded my military career with an honorable discharge. In the Army, I specialized in Airborne Artillery, a role markedly different from my current pursuit in the tech industry. My affinity for computers dates back to the '90s, when I first dabbled in programming with Visual Basic. Although becoming a professional developer once seemed beyond my reach, leaving the Army marked the turning point to follow my passion for technology.

At home, I am blessed with a wonderful family. My wife and I are proud parents of two incredible daughters. These days, my free time is less about gaming—a past passion—and more about coding and learning new things in technology. While I am naturally reserved in my personal life, I thrive on collaboration in professional settings, finding great satisfaction in working with others to bring projects to life.

Tennessee is where we currently call home, yet Florida, my home state, continues to hold a special place in my heart. Plans to return there hinge on securing a stable job that aligns with my aspirations. My educational journey culminated in a degree in Computer Science, underlining my belief in lifelong learning and adaptability. I am eager to demonstrate these qualities in a professional context. For a deeper insight into my career and skills, my resume is available for download from the 'Home' tab, or you can connect with me via the 'Contact' tab.
